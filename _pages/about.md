I am currently interested in working on the intersections between stochastic differential equations, deep learning, and their applications in finance. My focus is on leveraging SDEs to model financial market behaviors and using deep learning to enhance these models. This interdisciplinary approach aims to solve complex financial problems, such as risk management, portfolio optimization, and derivative pricing, by integrating advanced mathematical techniques with AI technologies.
